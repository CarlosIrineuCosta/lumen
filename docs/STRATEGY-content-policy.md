# Lumen - Content Policy and Moderation Guidelines

**FOR STRATEGIC DECISIONS ON CONTENT AND COMMUNITY STANDARDS**

## Platform Philosophy

Lumen is built on the principle that professional photography deserves a platform that respects artistic expression while maintaining quality standards. We believe in empowering creators, not censoring them based on arbitrary moral standards.

## Artistic Expression Standards

### Permitted Content

#### Artistic Nudity
- **Standard**: Maplethorpe-level artistic photography
- **Context**: Clear artistic intent and professional execution
- **Quality**: Focus on photographic merit, composition, lighting
- **Diversity**: All body types, genders, orientations welcome
- **Cultural**: Respect for diverse artistic traditions globally

#### Professional Photography Categories
- Fine art photography
- Portrait and fashion
- Dance and movement
- Yoga and wellness
- Tattoo and body art
- Creative and conceptual
- Editorial and documentary
- Beauty and glamour

### Prohibited Content

#### Hard Boundaries
- **Pornographic Content**: No penetration, insertion, or explicit sexual acts
- **Commercial Adult Services**: No OnlyFans promotion, escort services, cam sites
- **AI-Generated Images**: Zero tolerance, immediate warning then ban
- **Non-Photography**: No illustrations, paintings, digital art
- **Video Content**: Photography-only platform, no video uploads
- **Spam/Advertising**: No product placement, affiliate links, MLM

#### Content Patterns to Flag
- Minimal portfolio with website links
- Repetitive "DM for more" messaging
- Telegram/WhatsApp redirection
- "VIP content" references
- Payment platform promotions

## Moderation Framework

### Three-Strike System
1. **First Violation**: Private warning with specific policy reference
2. **Second Violation**: 7-day suspension with appeal option
3. **Third Violation**: Account termination with data export

### Appeal Process
- Users can appeal any moderation decision
- Human review within 48 hours
- Clear explanation of decision
- Opportunity to correct misunderstandings

### Quality Standards
- **Photographic Merit**: Technical quality matters
- **Artistic Intent**: Context and presentation considered
- **Professional Presentation**: Portfolio coherence valued
- **Community Contribution**: Engagement quality over quantity

## Geographic and Cultural Considerations

### International Standards
- **Primary Markets**: Brazil, Europe, Australia, USA, Japan
- **Cultural Sensitivity**: Respect local artistic traditions
- **Legal Compliance**: Operate within democratic jurisdictions
- **Language Support**: Multilingual moderation team planned

### Location-Based Policies
- **Europe**: GDPR compliance, liberal artistic standards
- **Brazil**: Strong artistic freedom protections
- **USA**: First Amendment considerations
- **Australia**: Balanced approach to artistic content
- **Japan**: Respect for unique aesthetic traditions

## User Reporting System

### Community Moderation
- **Paid Users as Moderators**: Quality gatekeeping by stakeholders
- **Weighted Reports**: Long-term users have more influence
- **Transparency**: Users see moderation queue status
- **Feedback Loop**: Reporters informed of outcomes

### Report Categories
- Not photography (AI, illustration)
- Commercial spam
- Explicit pornography
- Harassment or abuse
- Copyright infringement
- Underage content

## Special Considerations

### LGBTQ+ Content
- **Full Inclusion**: All orientations and identities welcome
- **Equal Standards**: Same artistic standards for all
- **Protection**: Zero tolerance for discriminatory reporting
- **Celebration**: Pride events and themed competitions

### Cultural and Religious Art
- **Respectful Inclusion**: Religious artistic photography welcome
- **Context Matters**: Historical and cultural documentation
- **Educational Value**: Anthropological photography protected
- **Sensitivity**: Balance between freedom and respect

## Enforcement Technology

### AI-Assisted Moderation
- **Initial Screening**: Vertex AI for obvious violations
- **Human Review**: All AI flags reviewed by humans
- **Pattern Detection**: Identify spam/promotional accounts
- **Quality Metrics**: Track false positive rates

### Account Verification
- **Professional Status**: Portfolio review for verification
- **Identity Confirmation**: Optional blue check system
- **Geographic Verification**: City-level location confirmation
- **Collaboration History**: Track professional relationships

## Edge Cases and Precedents

### Artistic vs Pornographic
- **Implied vs Explicit**: Suggestion allowed, explicit action not
- **Context Clues**: Setting, lighting, composition matter
- **Series Consideration**: View work as cohesive portfolio
- **Artist Statement**: Consider photographer's intent

### Borderline Content Examples
- **Allowed**: Implied intimacy, artistic suggestion, nude couples
- **Not Allowed**: Explicit sexual contact, genital focus, commercial fetish
- **Case-by-Case**: BDSM art, performance documentation, body modification
